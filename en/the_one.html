The One
Editorial Design

Made in Stellenbosch

The One is an iconic four-storey, fully-managed residential development consisting of 509 luxury apartments that features amongst others, a resort-style pool and themed courtyards, as well as a bespoke restaurant, coffee shop, and an on-site convenience store. Packed with a long list of incredible learning environments and amenities, The One is unlike any other development in the Western Cape, offering the ultimate luxury in private student residence.

Luxury appartments

Freelancing with the agency "Future Creative", my job was to produce a fancy A4 Brochure for the investors. In a very short space of time, I had to create a Look-and-Feel document which enhance the refined feeling of the Luxury building project. Then apply it into a 40 pages landscape format with Indesign, based on the editorial elements of the copywriter.
