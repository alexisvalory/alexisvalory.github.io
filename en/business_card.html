Business Card
Print

During the summer of 2015, I was looking for a 12-month work study contract in graphic and motion design (that I finally found at Drivy). So I made a business card for the different professionals I could meet.

About the inspiration, I found Noe Araujo's one. I really like the idea of self-definition. It reminded me of the back cover. So I checked the books on my desk and I chose one from Folio publishing house. Then I applied the same layout and typography. Yes I'm such a book!
