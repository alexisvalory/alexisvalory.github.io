L'Institut de l'Entreprise
Branding

Bringing the French closer to Businesses.

Created in 1975, l'Institut de l'Entreprise is a non-for-profit bringing together around a hundred companies that are multinational in scale but have a strong presence in France. The Institut works to promote the role and place of business in our society through a variety of programs:

  • L'Agora orchestrates collegial work between managers and stakeholders on the major transformations and mutations of the company.
  • L'IHEE accompanies and trains leaders from the corporate world and its stakeholders.
  • Melchior creates dialogue between the worlds of education and business to improve the professional integration of young people.
