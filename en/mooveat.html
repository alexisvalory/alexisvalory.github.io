MoovEat
UI Design

Halal & Food-Tech !

Mooveat is an application which offers you the time to have a meal! No need to be in advance or to phone, the mobile app gives you the availability in real-time and you can that way book a table, a meal and a number of invited people in just a few clicks.

Goal

How to improve the User Interface (=UI) of a delivery/booking table app in the Halal sector whilst still being unique from the other competitors?

Creative Solution

Firstly, I changed the colour palette by erasing the dark grey colour in the background and replaced it by white. I kept the green which is the famous colour of the app. For the card styles, I rounded them and added a light shadow. For the typography, I used Ubuntu Sans Serif (trendy in the UI world) and its contrasting fonts.
