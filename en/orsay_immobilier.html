Orsay Immobilier
Branding

30 years of cumulative experience

Orsay Immobilier is a real estate agency located near the Quai d'Orsay in Paris, in the street of the new Gainsbourg museum. They offer a turnkey experience, real assistance at each stage, advice and full-availability for better support. The company's goal is to create its own network in order to compete with French groups such as ORPI or FONCIA in Paris.

Several propositions

In order to meet their needs, I proposed different logos. Three were refused: one represented the window of a Parisian apartment with the letter "O" from Orsay, another had two keys as a symbol of freedom and independence, and a last revealed the astrological sign of Taurus for confidence and quality. To my (great) regret, the client preferred to choose a minimalist illustration of a bull found from a bank of icons... From that, I had to produce business cards and Powerpoint models while keeping the elegant style (shapes based on the curves of a Serif typeface) that I had explored in my other proposals.
