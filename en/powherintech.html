How to give a face and promote it on different supports?

My goal was to create a visual identity that could be adapted in different formats because in addition to making the layout of the guide, we had to promote it via visuals and videos, a type of "snack-content" on social networksand. INCO also made a website with the talented Alexia Toulmet and Emile Ledure). Helped by Garry Baptiste, I also filmed and edited learners who have taken this program.

Creative solution

In order for the target audience to identify with it, I wanted to remain very sober with a bold and capital typography, including the so-called feminine symbol. The colors were validated by the Chanel Foundation. For the image library, I opted for a "collage" style rich in diversity.
